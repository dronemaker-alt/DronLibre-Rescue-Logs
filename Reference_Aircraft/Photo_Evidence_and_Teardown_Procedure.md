## Teardown Documentation Workflow

1. Photograph exterior before disassembly.
2. Photograph screw locations.
3. Photograph each assembly layer.
4. Photograph all PCB markings.
5. Photograph connectors before disconnecting.
6. Record MCU part numbers.
7. Record radio subsystem identifiers.
8. Create findings linked to photographs.

## Rule
No engineering finding should exist without supporting evidence, photographs, measurements, or hardware identification.

This document establishes the DronLibre evidence chain process for future rescue, recovery, repair, and reverse-engineering activities.
