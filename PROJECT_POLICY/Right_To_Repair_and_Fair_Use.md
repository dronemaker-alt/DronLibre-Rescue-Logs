# DronLibre Project Policy
## Right to Repair, Fair Use, and Documentation Statement

### Mission
DronLibre exists to document, preserve, repair, analyze, and understand drone systems and related electronics through lawful engineering research and documentation.

---

## Right to Repair Statement

DronLibre supports the right of equipment owners to inspect, diagnose, maintain, repair, preserve, and restore hardware they legally own.

All documentation is intended for educational, research, maintenance, interoperability, archival, preservation, and repair purposes.

---

## Fair Use and Documentation Statement

References to trademarks, product names, manufacturers, firmware versions, photographs, hardware identifiers, and technical specifications are used solely for identification, commentary, criticism, comparison, repair, compatibility research, documentation, and educational purposes.

All trademarks, copyrights, and intellectual property remain the property of their respective owners.

---

## Independent Research Statement

Unless explicitly cited, all observations, measurements, photographs, diagrams, hardware maps, and findings represent original research performed by DronLibre contributors.

Documentation is based upon direct examination, testing, measurement, and engineering analysis.

---

## No Proprietary Material Distribution

DronLibre does not distribute proprietary firmware, copyrighted source code, encryption keys, authentication credentials, trade secrets, or confidential manufacturer intellectual property.

Documentation is limited to independently observed hardware behavior, publicly available information, repair procedures, interoperability research, and original engineering findings.

---

## Evidence-Based Documentation Policy

Engineering findings should be supported by photographs, measurements, hardware identification, oscilloscope captures, logic analysis, or direct observations whenever possible.

The goal of DronLibre is preservation of technical knowledge and support of lawful repair activities.
